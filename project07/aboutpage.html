FIONA HURLESS PHOTOGRAPHY


I've been gravitating towards cameras ever since a young age, and have considered photography both a passion and hobby for years. From my mom's small digital cameras to her DSLR used mainly for family pictures, I began to find myself getting my hands on any camera I could. After receiving a Canon Rebel T5 as a Christmas gift back in 2016, I began taking all kinds of pictures. While portrait photography remains my favorite, I've lugged my camera to many a party, vacation, or concert within reason to photograph a wide variety of shots. As a junior undergrad student at Syracuse University, my photography continues to be a hobby I am always looking to expand.
